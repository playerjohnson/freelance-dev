What Data I Collect

This site collects minimal data. If you submit the contact form, I receive your name, email address, and message via Formspree. This data is used solely to respond to your enquiry.

Cookies & Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data including pages visited, time on site, and referral source. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Analytics cookies are only loaded after you provide consent via the cookie banner. You can reject cookies and the site will function normally without them. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

Cookies used:
